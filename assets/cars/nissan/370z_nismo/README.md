# 2016 Nissan 370Z NISMO V2 low-poly visual asset

This directory contains original add-on geometry for the simplified 2016 Nissan 370Z NISMO V2 model used by the game.

The NISMO scene reuses the common Z34 cabin, glass, lamps and wheelbase geometry from the standard European 2016 370Z. These meshes replace the visual character of the front bumper, rear bumper, side skirts, rear wing, wheel finish and brake accents without copying a third-party model.

## Target proportions

- wheelbase: 2.550 m;
- overall body-kit length: approximately 4.410 m;
- width excluding mirrors: approximately 1.870 m;
- roof height: approximately 1.310 m;
- 19-inch staggered wheel package;
- front tyre reference: 245/40 R19;
- rear tyre reference: 285/35 R19.

## Composition

- `370z_nismo_2016_bodykit.obj` — extended GT-R-inspired front and rear fascias, side skirts and larger rear wing with supports;
- `370z_nismo_2016_trim.obj` — central grille, side intakes, splitter, diffuser, rear vents and skirt cores;
- `370z_nismo_2016_red_accents.obj` — front, side and rear NISMO accent lines, mirror accents and red brake calipers.

The kit is intentionally authored as overlapping closed volumes. Each visible add-on penetrates the common body shell or an adjacent NISMO component, preventing air gaps from appearing at gameplay camera distances while preserving an early-2000s racing-game polygon budget.

## Provenance

The geometry was constructed specifically for this repository from published exterior dimensions and public photographic references. It is not extracted from another game, scan or commercial asset. The meshes contain no manufacturer emblems or copied texture artwork and are covered by the repository license.
